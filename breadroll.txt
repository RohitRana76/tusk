Recipe to make breadroll
* purchase bread packet
*cut down the brown edges surrounded by white portion.
*boil potatoes in pressure cooker.
* squeeze the potatoes and add all the necessary in it, the same as make for aloo paratha
* Take one bread, sink it into very less water which you have taken in bowl or plate, quickly remove it once you dip it into it.
* Then press the wet bread with both of your hands tightly so that all the absorbed water can be removed.
* Fill bread with 1-2 spoons of potato masala and then give almost oval shaped to it with your hand.
* Boil the oil.
* Fry your bread roll
* Now your delicious bread roll is ready for eating
